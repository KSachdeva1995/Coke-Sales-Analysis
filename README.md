# Coke-Sales-Analysis

This project focuses on analyzing Coca-Cola sales data to gain actionable insights into sales performance, customer demand, and revenue trends. The analysis examines historical sales records across multiple dimensions such as time, region, product category, and sales channels to understand patterns and drivers of business growth.

Key objectives of the analysis include identifying top-performing products, evaluating regional and seasonal sales trends, tracking revenue and volume growth over time, and assessing the impact of pricing and promotions on overall sales. By applying data cleaning, aggregation, and visualization techniques, the project highlights key performance indicators (KPIs) such as total sales, average sales per period, growth rates, and contribution by product and region.

The insights derived from this analysis help stakeholders make data-driven decisions related to inventory planning, marketing strategies, demand forecasting, and operational efficiency. Overall, the Coke Sales Analysis provides a comprehensive view of business performance and supports strategic planning through clear, data-backed insights.
